Super Snake Adventure

Basic Controls

Arrow Keys: Control the snake's movement (Up, Down, Left, Right).

Boss Battle

Spacebar: Shoot bullets at the boss to inflict damage.

B Key: Plant a bomb near the boss. Bombs deal significant damage, but you have a limited supply!

Power-Ups

Collect power-ups to gain temporary advantages! Here's what each power-up does:

  • Invincibility (Yellow): Makes you temporarily immune to collisions.
  • Freeze (Blue): Freezes the boss in place.

X Key: Activate the currently held power-up.

Gameplay

Eat the food to grow longer and increase your score.

Avoid colliding with yourself, the walls, or the boss.

Defeat bosses to earn bonus points and power-ups.
